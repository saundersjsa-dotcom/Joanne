What happens on the call

This isn't a consultation where I nod along and tell you what you want to hear. It's 90 minutes of honest, strategic thinking about your brand — the kind of conversation that changes how you see your own content.

  • I review your current content (Instagram, website, Google presence) before we even get on the call — so we don't waste time on basics
  • We discuss your brand story, your audience, and what's not working — I ask the questions most creatives never think to ask
  • I give you specific, actionable recommendations: what to change, what to create, and what to stop doing
  • The call is recorded — you can revisit any insight whenever you need to
  • Within 48 hours, I send a concise follow-up email summarizing the key recommendations and your action plan

Who this is for

Brand owners who know something isn't working with their content but aren't sure what — and want expert guidance before investing in production. People who want to experience how I think before committing to a larger project. Brands considering a Brand Content Day who want to validate the direction first.

What this is not

This is not a free discovery call (that's separate and comes before this). This is not a deliverable-heavy package with documents and PDFs. And this is not a pitch for my other services — though many clients naturally choose to move forward after experiencing the depth of strategic thinking involved.
