Common questions

What makes this different from hiring a videographer?+
A videographer shows up and films what you tell them to. I show up having already spent a week understanding your brand — your story, your audience, what makes you different. The creative brief I build means every frame has strategic intent behind it. You get a creative director and a production team in one person.
Do you bring all the equipment?+
Yes. Camera, lenses, lighting, audio — I come fully equipped. You provide access to your space and your time. That's it.
What if I need content in all three languages?+
Two languages are included in the €2,400 price. The third language can be added for €200 — full native-quality captions and copy, not translations. Or upgrade to the Creative Retainer, which includes all three languages as standard.
What if I don't like how I look on camera?+
Almost every client says this before our first shoot. Part of my job is directing you in a way that feels natural, not performative. By the end of the day, you'll be surprised how comfortable it felt.
Where do you film?+
Anywhere in Belgium is included. For shoots elsewhere in Europe, I ask that you cover travel and accommodation. I've filmed across Belgium, the Netherlands, France, and South Africa.
What about the content license for ads?+
You own full rights for organic use across all your channels, permanently. Paid advertising usage (Meta ads, Google ads, etc.) is included for 12 months from delivery. After that, the ad license renews for a flat €300/year.
