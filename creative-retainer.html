Common questions

Why a 3-month minimum?+
Brand building takes time. Month 1 I'm learning your brand, month 2 we're in rhythm, month 3 the content starts compounding. Most clients stay much longer — but the minimum ensures we both see real results.
How does the emergency shoot work?+
Message me with at least 48 hours notice. Because I already know your brand, I can mobilize without the usual pre-production. Half-day shoot at €800, subject to calendar availability. This is exclusive to retainer clients — it's one of the key benefits of the ongoing partnership.
Can I cancel after 3 months?+
Yes, with 30 days notice. No penalties, no hard feelings. That said, most clients who reach month 3 see enough results to continue — the content gets better and the process gets easier every month.
How many retainer clients do you take on?+
Maximum 5 at any time. This ensures every brand gets my full creative attention. When I'm full, there's a waitlist.
Do I need to have done a Brand Content Day first?+
Not required, but most clients start with one to experience the process. It's a great way to see if we work well together before committing monthly.
What if I need to skip a month?+
Let me know 2 weeks in advance and we'll roll it to next month. One rollover per 6-month period, so the rhythm stays consistent.
