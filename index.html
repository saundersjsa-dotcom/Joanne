The creative behind the work

I didn't arrive here
in a straight line.
Nobody interesting does.

I'm Joanne, a Belgian creative with 8+ years across marketing, sales and creative production — and my own businesses along the way.

I work in English, French and Dutch — creating content that resonates in each language, not just translates. Based in Belgium, working across Europe.

My background in sales means I understand why people buy, not just what looks beautiful. Your content will be made to move people — strategically, emotionally, commercially.

I take on very few clients. And I give everything to the ones I do.

I
Strategy before camera
I never film without understanding your brand first. Every Brand Content Day starts with a strategic deep-dive — so the content has direction, not just aesthetics.
II
Three languages, one voice
Your brand should feel the same whether someone encounters it in English, French, or Dutch. I create content natively in all three — no awkward translations, no lost nuance.
III
Less, but better
I'd rather make one piece of content that genuinely moves someone than fifty that are forgotten. Impact is my measure, not volume.
IV
You work with me directly
No account managers. No junior teams. The person you speak to is the person making your content — with full creative investment in your brand.
